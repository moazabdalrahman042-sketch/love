We went Downtown, walked a lot, and grabbed a Pepsi. After that, we sat down for a bit, and I got her a beautiful red rose that looked just as lovely as her. Then, we ordered an Uber and headed to Zamalek. In the end, I walked her right to her doorstep because I wanted to make sure she was safe, and then I went home. We had such a wonderful time.
